What stays on your device

This page is a self-contained HTML file generated from your troop's BSA advancement export. All scout information — including names, patrol assignments, ages, rank progress, and merit badge completion records — is embedded directly in this file and exists only in your browser while the page is open.

This data is never transmitted to any server, analytics service, or third party. Closing the page or clearing your browser removes it entirely.

What is sent to Google Sheets (optional)

Google Sheets sync is entirely optional. If you choose to use it, the scheduler sends only:

  • BSA Member ID — a numeric identifier assigned by BSA
  • Class schedule selections — the badge or rank names and session times each scout has chosen

Scout names, patrol assignments, ages, and all other personal details are never included in data sent to Google Sheets.

Who controls the data

When sync is enabled, your troop admin creates and owns the Google Sheet. Only people the admin shares the Apps Script URL with can connect to that sheet. Google's standard data handling terms apply to data stored in your troop's Sheet.

ScoutSight does not operate any servers, does not receive sync data, and has no access to your troop's Google Sheet.

Using the app offline

Choosing Use Offline on the sync screen means no data is sent anywhere at all. All schedule selections remain in your browser only until you download them as a CSV or JSON file.

Downloaded CSV and JSON files contain BSA Member IDs and class selections only — the same information that would be sent to Google Sheets.
