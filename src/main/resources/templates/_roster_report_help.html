Roster Report — Optional

Adds additional information Scoutbook to the reports.

1
Go to advancements.scouting.org
2
Click Reports in the left navigation
3
At the top of the page click the Custom Reports tab
4
Click New Report and then New Custom Roster
5
Select the All Scouts checkbox on the left side. One the right side BSA Member ID must be selected. In addition, the system understands Current Rank Den/Patrol, Date Of Birth , Date Joined, School Info, and Grade
